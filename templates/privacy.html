4Donations and Financial Data

My donation platform is designed to be secure and transparent. When you make a contribution, your financial information is handled with the utmost care.

a. Payment Processing

I use industry-standard payment processors to handle transactions. I do not store your full credit card number or financial details on my servers. All payment information is encrypted and transmitted securely to the payment gateway.

b. Donation Methods

The following donation methods are supported:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Secure payments processed via Stripe.
  • PayPal: Redirects to PayPal for secure transaction processing.
  • Cryptocurrency: Anonymous donations via wallet-to-wallet transfers.

c. Data Handling

When you donate, I collect your name and email address to send a thank-you note and a receipt. This information is stored securely and is not shared with third parties, except as required by law. I do not use this information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.
