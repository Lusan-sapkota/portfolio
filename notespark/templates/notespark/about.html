What is NoteSpark?

NoteSpark is a modern, open-source note-taking app built for privacy, speed, and flexibility. It supports Markdown, works offline, and will soon feature a secure vault for sensitive notes. Licensed under GPLv3.

Created by Lusan Sapkota. Contributions welcome!
